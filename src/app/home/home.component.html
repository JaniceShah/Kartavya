GATE 2020

GATE 2020 will be organized by Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi in online mode tentatively on February 1, 2, 8 and 9. GATE 2020 will be conducted in 24 papers including statistics which was introduced in 2019. The entrance exam is a computer based one held in two sessions per day.
